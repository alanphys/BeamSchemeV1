IFA TypeÂ¶

Select the In Field Area (IFA) type in the box next to this key. BeamScheme will use this IFA definition in the calculation of parameters that use the IFA. This is a drop down selection field. Possible IFA Types are:

  • Proportional In Field Area
  • Circular In Field Area
  • Square In Field Area

The In Field Area affects the calculation of some parameters.
