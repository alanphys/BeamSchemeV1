1D Uniformity ICRUÂ¶

This is the normalised average between the maximum and minimum profile values taken over the in field area (IFA) normalised to the average of the IFA as defined by ICRU 72 eq 3.2:

\[{100} \cdot \cfrac {max - min} {ave}\]

Where max and min are the profile maximum and minimum respectively and ave is the average of the profile values in the IFA.

Protocol invocation name: 1D Uniformity ICRU

The max, min and ave may be affected by normalisation or windowing.
