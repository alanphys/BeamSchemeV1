Photon Profile Parameters

The defined photon parameters are:

  • Field Centre

  • Field Size

  • Penumbra 80%-20% Left

  • Penumbra 80%-20% Right

  • Area Symmetry

  • Dose Difference

  • MAX/CAX

Electron Profile Parameters

The electron parameters are the same as the photon parameters

Flattened field

BeamScheme uses 80% of the field size as the flattened area.
