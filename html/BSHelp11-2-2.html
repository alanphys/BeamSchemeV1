1D Interpolated ParametersÂ¶

Linear interpolation is performed between nearest neighbour data points to determine the relevant parameter. These are the traditional beam parameters

Available interpolated parameters are:

  • 1D Field Edge Left 50
  • 1D Field Edge Right 50
  • 1D Field Centre 50
  • 1D Field Size 50
  • 1D Penumbra 8020 Left
  • 1D Penumbra 8020 Right
  • 1D Penumbra 9010 Left
  • 1D Penumbra 9010 Right
  • 1D Penumbra 9050 Left
  • 1D Penumbra 9050 Right
