This protocol is recommended for high resolution devices such as EPID images.

Photon Profile Parameters

FFF parameters only apply to photon beams:

  • Field Edge Left

  • Field Edge Right

  • Field Centre

  • Field Size

  • Top position

  • Area Symmetry

  • Peak Slope Left

  • Peak Slope Right

  • Peak Slope Ratio

Flattened field

FFF beams use the concept of In Field Area which is primarily used for the Area Symmetry and Peak Slope calculations.
