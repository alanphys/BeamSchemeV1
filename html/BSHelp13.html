Technical Assistance¶

There are, of course, no bugs in the software. However, it may happen that the software does not behave in a manner you expect or generates results that you think are wrong. If so, we would like to know about it. Likewise, if you feel BeamScheme should be doing something that it isn’t, please tell us at:

https://github.com/alanphys/BeamSchemeV1/issues

or contact the authors on alanphys@yenzakahle.co.za.
