Photon Profile Parameters

The defined photon parameters are:

  • Field Centre

  • Field Size

  • Penumbra 80%-20% Left

  • Penumbra 80%-20% Right

  • Maximum Dose Ratio

  • Mean Value

  • Maximum Variation

Electron Profile Parameters

The defined electron parameters are:

  • Field Centre

  • Field Size

  • Penumbra 80%-20% Left

  • Penumbra 80%-20% Right

  • Maximum Dose Ratio

  • Flatness L90/L50

  • Maximum Variation

Flattened field

The flattend field is defined by the In Field Area.
