These metrics express the degree of unflatness or nonuniformity in the profile.

Available flatness and uniformity parameters are:

  • 1D Flatness Ave
  • 1D Flatness Diff
  • 1D Flatness Ratio
  • 1D Flatness CAX
  • 1D Uniformity ICRU
  • 1D Uniformity NCS
  • 1D Flatness 9050
  • 1D Peak Slope Left
  • 1D Peak Slope Right
  • 1D Peak Slope Ratio
